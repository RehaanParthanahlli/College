**JetBrains is the company, and IntelliJ IDEA is one of its products. They are not the same—JetBrains makes many developer tools, while IntelliJ IDEA is their flagship IDE for Java and other JVM languages.**  

---

## 🔑 Key Distinction
- **JetBrains** → The software company that develops multiple IDEs and tools (e.g., IntelliJ IDEA, PyCharm, WebStorm, Rider, CLion).
- **IntelliJ IDEA** → A specific IDE created by JetBrains, mainly focused on **Java, Kotlin, and other JVM languages**, with support for many frameworks and plugins.

---

## 🛠 JetBrains Product Ecosystem
JetBrains offers a wide range of IDEs, each tailored for different programming needs:
- **IntelliJ IDEA** → Best for Java, Kotlin, Scala, Groovy, and enterprise frameworks like Spring, Hibernate, Jakarta EE.
- **PyCharm** → Specialized for Python and data science workflows.
- **WebStorm** → Focused on JavaScript, TypeScript, and modern web frameworks (React, Angular, Vue).
- **CLion** → For C and C++ development.
- **Rider** → For .NET and C# development.
- **PhpStorm, RubyMine, GoLand** → For PHP, Ruby, and Go respectively.  [JetBrains](https://www.jetbrains.com/products/compare/?product=idea&product=idea-ult)

👉 If you’re mainly coding in **Java/Kotlin**, IntelliJ IDEA Community is often enough. For **enterprise projects** or multi-language development, IntelliJ IDEA Ultimate is recommended.

---

## ⚖️ Common Confusion
- People often say “JetBrains” when they mean “IntelliJ IDEA,” because IntelliJ is JetBrains’ most popular product.
- But JetBrains is the **umbrella company**, and IntelliJ IDEA is just one of its many IDEs.  
- Example: PyCharm (Python IDE) is also made by JetBrains, but it’s not IntelliJ IDEA.  [SelectHub](https://www.selecthub.com/integrated-development-environment-solutions/pycharm-vs-intellij-idea/)

---

## 🚀 Quick Takeaway
- **JetBrains ≠ IntelliJ IDEA.**  
- JetBrains is the company; IntelliJ IDEA is its flagship IDE.  
- If you’re working with **Java/Kotlin**, IntelliJ IDEA is the go-to. If you’re working with **Python**, PyCharm is better.
